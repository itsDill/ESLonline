üí≠ Deep Dive Discussion Questions

  • What's your ideal weekend morning routine? How does it set the tone for your day?
  • Do you prefer spontaneous weekend adventures or carefully planned activities? Why?
  • Describe your most memorable weekend experience. What made it so special?
  • How do weekend traditions differ between cultures? Share examples from your background.
  • If you could design the perfect 48-hour weekend, what would it include?
  • Do you believe weekends are essential for mental health and productivity? Explain your thoughts.

  • How has your idea of a perfect weekend changed as you've gotten older?
