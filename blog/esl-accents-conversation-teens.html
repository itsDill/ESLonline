Accent Personality Guide

Every English accent has its own vibe and personality. Here's the real tea on major English accents:

🇺🇸
American English
"That's so cool, dude!"
R-sounds strong, casual tone
Confident & Direct
🇬🇧
British English (RP)
"That's brilliant, mate!"
Clear consonants, dropped R's
Polite & Articulate
🇦🇺
Australian English
"That's heaps good, mate!"
Rising intonation, vowel shifts
Laid-back & Friendly
🇨🇦
Canadian English
"That's wicked awesome, eh?"
'About' sounds like 'aboot'
Friendly & Apologetic
🏴󠁧󠁢󠁳󠁣󠁴󠁿
Scottish English
"That's pure dead brilliant!"
Rolled R's, unique vocabulary
Warm & Expressive
🇮🇪
Irish English
"That's class, so it is!"
Musical intonation, soft consonants
Musical & Charming
🇿🇦
South African English
"That's lekker, hey!"
Afrikaans influence, unique slang
Energetic & Unique
🇳🇿
New Zealand English
"That's choice as, bro!"
'I' sounds like 'E', short vowels
Relaxed & Nature-loving
