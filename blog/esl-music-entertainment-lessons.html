🎬 Movie & TV Vocabulary

Film Industry:

  • Director - person who controls the making of a film
  • Producer - person who manages the film's finances
  • Screenplay - the script of a movie
  • Cinematography - the art of filming
  • Soundtrack - music in a movie
  • Box Office - movie ticket sales

TV Terms:

  • Episode - one part of a TV series
  • Season - collection of episodes
  • Pilot - first episode of a new series
  • Finale - last episode of a season/series
  • Cliffhanger - suspenseful ending
  • Binge-watch - watch many episodes in a row

📝 Comprehensive Exercises

� Speaking Practice: Music Festival Experience

Practice speaking about music festivals! Use the questions below as conversation starters with a partner or record yourself answering them.

🎪 Festival Discussion Questions

1. Describe a music festival you've been to or would like to attend. What makes it special?

2. What's your ideal music festival lineup? Name 5 artists you'd want to see.

3. Compare different music genres at festivals. Which atmosphere do you prefer and why?

4. What should someone bring to a music festival? Give advice to a first-timer.
