Excellent points everyone! Here's the complete Band 7+ roadmap:
Part 1: Be Conversational Yet Detailed
Don't just answer "Yes, I like music." Try: "Absolutely! I'm particularly drawn to jazz because it allows for such creative improvisation..."
Part 2: Structure is Everything
Use the 1-minute prep time to create a clear outline. Follow: Context → Details → Personal Impact → Conclusion
Part 3: Think Like an Examiner
Show critical thinking with phrases like "There are several factors to consider..." or "This is a complex issue because..."

Vary Your Intonation

Think Aloud Strategy
