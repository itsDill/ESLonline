ğŸ“š Grammar Focus: Imperatives & Sequence Markers

Imperatives: Commands that tell someone what to do

Examples: "Peel the potatoes." "Stir the sauce." "Bake for 20 minutes."

Sequence Markers: Words that show order and steps

Examples: First, Then, Next, After that, Finally
