Part 1: Let's Learn Some Cool Words!

Easy Grammar Tips for Speaking

Talking About Now (Present Simple)
I play video games every day.
Use this to talk about things you do all the time!
Talking About Later (Future)
I will go to the park tomorrow.
Use "will" to talk about things you're going to do!
Talking About Before (Past)
I ate pizza yesterday.
Use past tense to talk about things that already happened!
Can & Can't (Ability)
I can swim but I can't fly.
Use "can" for things you're able to do, and "can't" for things you can't do!

Part 2: Tongue Twisters Challenge! 👅

Can You Say These Super-Fast?

Try saying each one slowly first, then faster and faster! Click the checkbox when you can say it 3 times fast! 🏃‍♂️💨

"She sells seashells by the seashore" 🐚
Listen to the "s" and "sh" sounds!
"Red lorry, yellow lorry" 🚛
Be careful with "r" and "l"!
"Peter Piper picked a peck of pickled peppers" 🫑
So many "p" sounds!
"How much wood would a woodchuck chuck if a woodchuck could chuck wood?" 🪵
This one is super tricky!
"Fuzzy Wuzzy was a bear, Fuzzy Wuzzy had no hair" 🐻
Watch those "z" sounds!
"Betty Botter bought some butter" 🧈
Practice your "b" sound!
"Toy boat, toy boat, toy boat" 🚤
Short but super hard!
"A proper copper coffee pot" ☕
Don't mix up those sounds!
"Six sticky skeletons" 💀
"S" and "k" together!
"Eleven benevolent elephants" 🐘
So many "e" sounds!

Practice These Tricky Sounds!

TH Sounds 👅
Think, thank, this, that, three, thunder
Put your tongue between your teeth and blow!
R vs. L Sounds 🎵
Right/light, read/lead, rice/lice
R: curl your tongue back. L: touch the roof of your mouth!
SH Sound 🤫
Ship, shop, fish, wash, brush
Make the "shhhh" sound like when you want someone to be quiet!
CH Sound 🍒
Chair, cheese, church, beach, lunch
Like a train sound: "ch-ch-ch"!
