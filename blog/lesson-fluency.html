Pronunciation Practice

Master these important sounds to speak more clearly and confidently!

/TH/ Sound Put your tongue between your teeth!

Try these words:

Think
Thank
Three
Mother
Brother
Weather

Practice sentence: "I think my mother and brother like this weather on Thursday."

/R/ Sound Curl your tongue back slightly!

Try these words:

Red
Round
Rainbow
Rabbit
Ready
Really

Practice sentence: "The red rabbit ran really fast around the rainbow."

/L/ Sound Touch your tongue to the roof of your mouth!

Try these words:

Like
Love
Little
Lemon
Letter

Practice sentence: "I love the little yellow lemon on the table."

/V/ vs /B/ Bite your bottom lip for /V/!

üìñ Story Tips

  • Don't worry about perfect grammar - focus on telling the story!
  • Use descriptive words (colors, sizes, feelings)
  • Include a beginning, middle, and end
  • Add dialogue: "The character said..."
  • Make it fun and creative!

Building Speaking Confidence

‚ú® Remember These Tips!

  • It's okay to make mistakes - that's how we learn!
  • Speak slowly and clearly, don't rush
  • Use gestures and expressions to help communicate
  • If you don't know a word, try to explain it another way
  • Practice a little bit every day
  • Have fun and enjoy speaking English!
