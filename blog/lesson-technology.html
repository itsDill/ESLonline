📚 Grammar Focus: Technology in Our Lives

Present Simple Tense

We use present simple to talk about habits and things we do regularly with technology.

✓ I use my computer every day.

Regular habit with technology

✓ She plays games on her tablet.

Third person singular - add 's'

✓ They watch videos on the internet.

Plural subject - no 's'

⚠️ Remember!
  • He/She/It → add -s (uses, plays, watches)
  • I/You/We/They → no change (use, play, watch)

Can / Can't for Abilities

Use can and can't to talk about what technology can do.

✓ Can (Positive)
  • A robot can clean the floor.
  • I can send emails.
  • We can watch movies online.
✗ Can't (Negative)
  • A robot can't feel emotions.
  • I can't use it without WiFi.
  • We can't play without charging.
