Practice these questions with your teacher or classmates!

At Home

  • Do you use a computer at home?
  • What is your favorite app?
  • How do you use the internet?

Future Tech

  • Would you like a robot friend?
  • What will technology be like in 10 years?
  • Do you want a flying car?
  • What's the coolest gadget ever?
