‚è∞ Past Tense (What I Did)

Use past tense to talk about trips you already finished.

"Last summer, I visited Paris with my family."
"We stayed in a beautiful hotel near the Eiffel Tower."
"I took many photos of famous landmarks."

Fill in the blanks with past tense verbs:

Yesterday, I ___ to London.

We ___ fish and chips for lunch.

My sister ___ a souvenir at the gift shop.

üîÆ Future Tense (What I Will Do)

Use future tense to talk about trips you plan to take.

"Next month, I will visit Tokyo with my school."
"We are going to see cherry blossoms in the park."
"I will try sushi for the first time!"

Fill in the blanks with future tense:

Tomorrow, we ___ to New York.

I ___ the Statue of Liberty.

My parents ___ photos of the city.
