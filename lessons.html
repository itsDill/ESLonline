Today's grammar focus is on the present perfect tense - learn how to use it correctly in various contexts.

Present Perfect Tense

Use the present perfect to talk about:

  • Experiences (I have visited Paris)
  • Actions that started in the past and continue now (She has lived here for 5 years)
  • Recent actions with present results (I've lost my keys)

Structure: Subject + have/has + past participle
Example: "They have finished their homework already."
