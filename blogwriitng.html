Writing Checklists

Pre-Writing Checklist
  • Define your primary purpose and audience
  • Research and gather supporting evidence
  • Create an outline or structure
  • Choose appropriate tone and style
  • Set realistic length and deadline goals
  • Eliminate distractions in writing environment
  • Warm up with freewriting or brainstorming
  • Review any style guides or requirements
  • Gather relevant examples and quotes
  • Clarify key terms and concepts
First Draft Checklist
  • Write continuously without editing
  • Focus on getting ideas down first
  • Include all main points from outline
  • Write a compelling opening hook
  • Use transitions between paragraphs
  • Support claims with evidence
  • Address potential counterarguments
  • Write a strong conclusion
  • Don't worry about perfection
  • Save work frequently
Revision Checklist
  • Read entire piece aloud
  • Check logical flow and organization
  • Strengthen weak transitions
  • Eliminate unnecessary words and phrases
  • Vary sentence length and structure
  • Replace weak verbs with strong ones
  • Ensure consistent tone throughout
  • Verify all facts and citations
  • Check paragraph unity and coherence
  • Strengthen opening and closing
Final Polish Checklist
  • Proofread for grammar and spelling
  • Check punctuation and capitalization
  • Verify proper formatting and style
  • Ensure consistent verb tenses
  • Check subject-verb agreement
  • Review word choice for precision
  • Eliminate clich√©s and redundancies
  • Verify proper citation format
  • Check for unintentional plagiarism
  • Get feedback from trusted readers
