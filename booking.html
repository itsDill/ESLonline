Booking Process

Fill in the form with your desired course details, get a quote, and we'll contact you to confirm availability and schedule your first lesson.

Payment Options

We offer flexible payment options including bank transfer, credit card, or PayPal. Payment is due before your first lesson.

Scheduling

Classes can be scheduled Monday through Sunday from 8:00 to 21:00, subject to teacher availability. We'll do our best to accommodate your preferences.

Free Assessment

All new students receive a free 15-minute assessment to determine your current English level and learning goals before your first paid lesson.

Cancellation Policy

Lessons can be cancelled or rescheduled with at least 24 hours notice. Late cancellations or no-shows will be charged the full lesson fee.

Learning Materials

All necessary learning materials are included in your course fee and will be provided digitally before each lesson.
