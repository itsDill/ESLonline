Try saying these phrases in different accents:

American: "I'm going to the store to buy some water."
Pronunciation: Hard 'r' sounds, flat 'a' in 'water'
British: "I'm going to the shop to buy some water."
Pronunciation: Silent 'r' sounds, 'wah-tah' for water
Australian: "I'm going to the shops to buy some water, mate."
Pronunciation: Rising intonation, shortened vowels
Canadian: "I'm going to the store to buy some water, eh?"
Pronunciation: Similar to American but with 'about' sounds like 'aboot'
