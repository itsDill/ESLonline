🏆 Essential Sports Vocabulary

Warm up
prepare your body before exercise
Cool down
gentle exercise after intense activity
Personal best (PB)
your best individual performance
Endurance
ability to exercise for long periods
Stamina
physical and mental strength
Teamwork
working together effectively
Fair play
playing honestly and respectfully
Get in shape
become physically fit
Break a record
achieve a better performance than before
Home team advantage
benefit of playing in familiar environment
Spectator
person who watches a sporting event
Championship
competition to determine the best

💬 Sports Discussion Questions

  • What's your favorite sport to play? What do you enjoy most about it?
  • Do you prefer individual sports or team sports? Explain your preference.
  • How important is winning versus having fun when playing sports?
  • What life lessons can sports teach us? Give specific examples.
  • Should schools make physical education mandatory? Why or why not?
  • How do sports bring different cultures and countries together?
  • What's the most exciting sporting event you've ever watched or attended?
  • Do you think professional athletes are good role models? Discuss.
  • How has technology changed modern sports? Give examples.
  • What sport would you like to try that you've never done before?

📝 Sports Interview Project

Your Mission: Interview someone about their sports experience! Ask about their favorite sport, memorable moments, and what sports mean to them. Write a 200-word report using at least 8 vocabulary words from this lesson.


Bonus Challenge: Research a sport that's popular in another country but uncommon in yours. Present 3 interesting facts about it to the class!


Creative Option: Design your own sport! Explain the rules, equipment needed, and why it would be fun to play.
