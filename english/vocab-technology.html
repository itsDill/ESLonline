Learn Technology & Digital Vocabulary

Expand your vocabulary for computers, internet, devices, and modern technology. Essential words for navigating the digital world.

Digital Age Tip

Technology vocabulary is constantly evolving! Learning these terms helps you use devices, browse the internet, communicate online, and stay current with digital trends. Start with everyday tech you use most!

Laptop
/ˈlæp.tɑːp/
A portable computer that you can carry and use anywhere
"I use my laptop for work and school."
Wi-Fi
/ˈwaɪ.faɪ/
Wireless internet connection technology
"Can I have the Wi-Fi password?"
Download
/ˈdaʊn.loʊd/
To copy data from the internet to your device
"Download the app from the app store."
Click
/klɪk/
To press a button on a computer mouse
"Click the button to submit the form."
Website
/ˈweb.saɪt/
A collection of web pages on the internet
"Visit our website for more information."
Post
/poʊst/
To publish content on social media or a website
"I posted a photo on Instagram."

Tech Learning Tips

Effective strategies to master technology vocabulary

Change Device Language

Set your phone, computer, and apps to English. This exposes you to tech vocabulary daily and helps you learn naturally.

Watch Tech Reviews

Watch English tech reviews and unboxing videos on YouTube. Learn how native speakers describe technology and features.

Read Tech Articles

Follow English tech blogs and news sites. Read about new gadgets, apps, and digital trends to expand your vocabulary.

Use English Apps

Download and use popular apps in English. Navigate menus, read notifications, and follow instructions to practice daily.

Join Online Communities

Participate in English tech forums and social media groups. Discuss technology, ask questions, and learn from others.

Learn Acronyms

Study common tech acronyms like USB, CPU, RAM, GPS, and AI. These appear frequently in technology discussions.

  • • The app isn't working.
  • • I can't connect to Wi-Fi.
  • • My phone is frozen.
  • • The battery died.
  • • It won't turn on.

  • • Log out / Sign out
  • • Upload a file
  • • Save the document
  • • Delete this message
  • • Restart the computer

Online Communication

  • • Share the link
  • • Tag me in the photo

  • • Subscribe to the channel
