IELTS

International English Language Testing System - Your gateway to study and work opportunities worldwide.

  • Speaking, Listening, Reading, Writing modules
  • Academic & General Training versions
  • Band score 0-9 system
  • Valid for 2 years
  • 2 hours 45 minutes total

TOEIC

Test of English for International Communication - Essential for business and workplace English proficiency.

  • Listening & Reading sections
  • Speaking & Writing (optional)
  • Score range 10-990
  • Valid for 2 years
  • 2 hours total (L&R)

EIKEN

Society for Testing English Proficiency - Japan's most popular English proficiency test with 7 grade levels.

  • 7 levels: Grade 5 to Grade 1
  • Primary test + Interview
  • Pass/Fail system
  • Lifetime validity
  • Variable duration by level

Essential Study Tips

Create a Study Schedule

Dedicate 1-2 hours daily across all four skills. Use our structured lesson plans to track progress and stay motivated throughout your preparation journey.

Practice Listening Daily

Listen to English podcasts, news, and audio materials daily to improve comprehension and familiarize yourself with different accents.

Read Extensively

Read newspapers, academic articles, and books to improve vocabulary and reading speed. Focus on understanding main ideas and details.

Speaking Practice

Practice speaking with native speakers or language partners. Record yourself to identify areas for improvement.

Writing Skills

Practice different types of writing tasks. Focus on structure, grammar, and vocabulary range. Get feedback when possible.

Master Time Management

Take practice tests under real exam conditions. Learn to allocate time effectively across sections and develop strategies for different question types.
