Proven Learning Strategies

Spaced Repetition

Review words at increasing intervals to move them into long-term memory. Our system automatically schedules reviews for optimal retention.

Create Associations

Link new words to familiar concepts, images, or personal experiences to create stronger memory connections.

Learn in Context

Study words within sentences and real-world situations rather than in isolation for better understanding and usage.

Daily Practice

Consistent daily practice, even for just 10-15 minutes, is more effective than long, irregular study sessions.
