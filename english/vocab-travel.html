Learn Travel & Tourism Vocabulary

Expand your vocabulary for airports, hotels, transportation, and sightseeing. Essential words for confident travel in English.

Travel Smart Tip

Knowing travel vocabulary helps you navigate airports, book hotels, ask for directions, and communicate with locals. Practice these phrases before your trip to travel with confidence!

Passport
/ˈpæs.pɔːrt/
An official document that allows you to travel to foreign countries
"Don't forget to bring your passport to the airport."
Boarding Pass
/ˈbɔːr.dɪŋ ˌpæs/
A ticket that allows you to board a plane
"Please show your boarding pass at the gate."
Reservation
/ˌrez.ɚˈveɪ.ʃən/
A booking or arrangement made in advance for a hotel room or service
"I have a reservation under the name Smith."
Tourist
/ˈtʊr.ɪst/
A person who is visiting a place for pleasure
"The city is full of tourists during the summer."
Taxi / Cab
/ˈtæk.si/ /kæb/
A car with a driver you pay to take you somewhere
"Let's take a taxi to the hotel."
Luggage / Baggage
/ˈlʌɡ.ɪdʒ/ /ˈbæɡ.ɪdʒ/
Bags and suitcases used for carrying belongings while traveling
"Please collect your luggage from baggage claim."

Travel Learning Tips

Master travel vocabulary with these strategies

Study Before Your Trip

Learn essential travel phrases weeks before traveling. Focus on vocabulary specific to your destination and planned activities.

Practice Key Phrases

Memorize important phrases like "Where is...?", "How much does this cost?", and "Can you help me?" for common travel situations.

Use Travel Apps

Download English travel apps for translation, navigation, and local tips. Practice using them before your trip.

Watch Travel Vlogs

Watch English travel vlogs and documentaries about your destination to hear authentic travel vocabulary in context.

Learn Direction Words

Master words for directions like left, right, straight, north, south, east, west to navigate confidently.

Make a Travel Phrasebook

Create a personal phrasebook with translations of phrases you'll need. Keep it handy during your travels.

Essential Travel Phrases

Must-know expressions for traveling

At the Airport

  • • Where is check-in?
  • • Which gate is my flight?
  • • Where is baggage claim?
  • • Is this flight delayed?
  • • I'd like a window seat.
  • • Where is customs?

At the Hotel

  • • I have a reservation.
  • • Can I check in early?
  • • What time is checkout?
  • • Do you have Wi-Fi?
  • • Where is the elevator?
  • • Can I have extra towels?

Getting Around

  • • How do I get to...?
  • • Where is the nearest...?
  • • Can you show me on the map?
  • • How much is a ticket?
  • • Does this bus go to...?
  • • I'm lost. Can you help?
