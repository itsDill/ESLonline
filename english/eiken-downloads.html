Grammar Cheat Sheet - All Grades

Eiken Grammar Cheat Sheet

Complete grammar reference for all Eiken grades.

Verb Tenses

Present Simple

Form: Subject + base verb (add -s/-es for he/she/it)

Use: Habits, facts, schedules

I study English every day.
She works at a hospital.

Past Simple

Form: Subject + verb-ed (or irregular past form)

Use: Completed actions in the past

I studied yesterday.
She went to school.

Present Perfect

Form: Subject + have/has + past participle

Use: Past actions connected to now, experience

I have studied English for 5 years.
She has been to America.

Conditionals

First Conditional (Real/Possible Future)

Form: If + present simple, will + base verb

If I study hard, I will pass the test.

Second Conditional (Unreal/Hypothetical)

Form: If + past simple, would + base verb

If I had money, I would travel.

Passive Voice

Form: Subject + be + past participle

Present: The letter is written.

Past: The letter was written.

Perfect: The letter has been written.

Modal Verbs

can - ability, permission: I can speak English.

must - obligation: You must study.

should - advice: You should eat more vegetables.

may/might - possibility: It may rain today.

4-Week Study Planner

Eiken 4-Week Study Planner

Print this planner and check off each task as you complete it.

Week 1: Vocabulary Foundation

Day 1: Learn 20 new vocabulary words, write each in a sentence
Day 2: Learn 20 new words, review yesterday's words with flashcards
Day 3: Learn 20 new words, reading practice (20 min)
Day 4: Learn 20 new words, listening practice (15 min)
Day 5: Complete vocabulary test, review incorrect answers
Day 6: Review all 100 words, practice reading passage
Day 7: REST - Watch English content (30 min)

Week 2: Grammar Focus

Day 8: Review verb tenses, complete grammar exercises (20 questions)
Day 9: Study question formation, practice asking/answering
Day 10: Study conditional sentences, grammar exercises
Day 11: Study comparatives/superlatives, writing practice
Day 12: Study passive voice, grammar review test
Day 13: Mixed grammar practice, full reading section
Day 14: REST - Reflect on progress

Week 3: Reading & Listening

Day 15: Skimming practice, timed reading (2 passages)
Day 16: Scanning practice, listening (dialogue type)
Day 17: Reading comprehension questions, time management
Day 18: Full reading section practice test
Day 19: Full listening section practice test
Day 20: Combined practice, review wrong answers
Day 21: REST - Prepare for final week

Week 4: Practice Tests & Review

Day 22: Full practice test (timed), score and review
Day 23: Focus on weak areas, extra practice
Day 24: Second full practice test, compare with first
Day 25: Review all vocabulary and grammar
Day 26: Light review only, relax
Day 27: Rest well, prepare test materials
Day 28: TEST DAY - You've got this!
