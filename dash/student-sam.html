Football Vocabulary

Striker
A forward player whose main job is to score goals
"The striker scored a hat-trick in the match!"
Penalty Kick
A free shot at goal from 12 yards, awarded for a foul in the box
"The referee awarded a penalty kick for the handball."
Offside
When an attacking player is ahead of the last defender when the ball is played
"The goal was disallowed because the player was offside."
Clean Sheet
When a team doesn't concede any goals in a match
"The goalkeeper kept a clean sheet for the fifth match in a row!"
Tackle
Taking the ball away from an opponent using feet
"The defender made a brilliant tackle to stop the attack."
Free Kick
An uncontested kick awarded after a foul
"He scored directly from a free kick outside the box!"
Through Ball
A pass played between defenders for a teammate to run onto
"The midfielder played a perfect through ball to the striker."
Dribbling
Moving with the ball while maintaining control
"His dribbling skills helped him get past three defenders!"

Geography Vocabulary

Archipelago
A group or chain of islands clustered together
"Indonesia is the largest archipelago in the world."
Plateau
A flat, elevated area of land higher than the surrounding area
"The Tibetan Plateau is the highest plateau on Earth."
Peninsula
A piece of land surrounded by water on three sides
"Italy is shaped like a peninsula jutting into the Mediterranean."
Latitude
The distance north or south of the equator, measured in degrees
"The equator is at 0 degrees latitude."
Canyon
A deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often carved by a river
"The Grand Canyon is one of the most famous canyons in the world."
Monsoon
A seasonal wind pattern bringing heavy rainfall
"The monsoon season brings much-needed rain to India."
Tundra
A vast, flat, treeless Arctic region with permanently frozen soil
"Polar bears live in the Arctic tundra."
Delta
A triangular area where a river splits before entering the sea
"The Nile Delta is extremely fertile and supports agriculture."

Football Idioms & Expressions

"Move the goalposts"
To change the rules or requirements unfairly
"They keep moving the goalposts every time I meet the target!"
"Ball is in your court"
It's your turn to take action or make a decision
"I've done my part, now the ball is in your court."
"Score an own goal"
To do something that harms yourself by mistake
"He scored an own goal by criticizing the boss in the meeting."
"On the ball"
Alert, quick to understand or react
"She's really on the ball when it comes to solving problems."

Amazing Geography Facts

🌊 Deepest Ocean Point
The Mariana Trench is 11,034 meters deep - deeper than Mount Everest is tall!
"Only three people have ever reached the bottom of the Mariana Trench."
🏜️ Driest Place on Earth
The Atacama Desert in Chile has areas that haven't seen rain for over 400 years
"The Atacama Desert is even drier than Antarctica!"
🌍 Shrinking Sea
The Dead Sea is shrinking by about 1 meter per year
"You can float effortlessly in the Dead Sea because it's so salty!"
🏔️ Growing Mountain
Mount Everest grows about 4mm taller every year due to tectonic plates
"The Himalayas are still being pushed up by plate movements!"

Speaking Practice Prompts

Practice speaking out loud! Record yourself or talk with a friend about these topics:

⚽ Football Discussion

  • Describe your favorite football player and explain why they're special
  • Talk about an exciting match you watched recently
  • If you could play any position, which would you choose and why?
  • Explain the offside rule to someone who doesn't know football

🌍 Geography Talk

  • Describe a country you'd love to visit and what you'd do there
  • Compare two different landforms (like mountains and deserts)
  • Explain why the equator is important for climate
  • Talk about the most amazing geographical feature you've learned about

🎯 Challenge Questions

  • If you could create a football team from any country, which country and why?
  • How does geography affect where football is most popular?
  • What vocabulary words did you learn today that you'll use in real life?
  • Describe your dream: combining football and world travel!
