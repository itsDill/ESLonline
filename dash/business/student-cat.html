🎤 Part B — Speaking Practice 10 Minutes

5 speaking tasks × 2 minutes each. Press Start Timer when ready for each task. Speak clearly and use vocabulary from the book.

SPEAKING TIMER
2:00
Task 1 of 5
🎤 Task 1 — Unit 1 · Jobs & Introductions  2 minutes
Introduce yourself professionally. Include:
• Your name and job title
• The company you work for and what it does
• Your main responsibilities (use present simple)
• One fact about where you are from or where your company is based
💡 Language tips: "I'm… / I work for… / My company sells… / I'm responsible for… / We're based in…"
🎤 Task 2 — Units 2 & 3 · Products, Services & Location  2 minutes
Describe your workplace. Talk about:
• What your company's main products or services are
• Where your office is located and what facilities are nearby
• What departments are in your building (use "there is / there are")
• Directions from the entrance to your desk or office
💡 Language tips: "There is a… on the… floor. / Turn left along… / It's opposite… / Our company provides…"
🎤 Task 3 — Units 5, 6 & 8 · Past Work & Experience  2 minutes
Talk about your work history and experience. Include:
• A past job or project (use past simple: "I worked… / I managed… / We launched…")
• Something you have done that relates to your career (present perfect: "I have worked… / I have attended…")
• A skill or qualification you have
💡 Language tips: "I worked at… for… years. / I have managed a team of… / In 20XX, I… / I have recently…"
🎤 Task 4 — Units 9 & 10 · Comparing & Opinions  2 minutes
Compare two things and give your opinion. Choose ONE:
• Compare two products you know (price, quality, design)
• Compare two companies (size, reputation, working culture)
• Discuss the pros and cons of working in a team vs. working alone

Use comparatives and superlatives, and give your opinion politely.
💡 Language tips: "In my opinion… / I think… / A is more… than B. / The best option is… / I prefer… because…"
🎤 Task 5 — Units 11 & 12 · Future Plans & Schedule  2 minutes
Talk about your future plans. Discuss:
• What you are going to do to improve your English in the next 3 months
• Any upcoming business trips or travel plans
• Your schedule for next week (use dates and times)
• One career goal you have for the next year
💡 Language tips: "I'm going to… / I'm planning to… / On Monday at… I have… / My goal is to… / I'd like to…"
