📗 Unit 2: Companies
📐 Grammar 💼 Vocabulary 🎧 Audio 💬 Conversation 📝 Review
📋 Unit 2 Overview — What You'll Learn ▼

🎯 Learning Goals

  • Talk about companies and what they do
  • Describe departments and where people work
  • Use the present simple for facts and routines
  • Ask about company structure, size, and location

📚 What's Covered

  • Grammar: present simple, third person -s
  • Vocabulary: company types, departments, workplace words, and office locations
  • Audio: real Unit 2 listening tracks from Activity 2
  • Communication: describing your company
💡 Teacher note: This unit is designed for a 3-hour block. Start with company vocabulary, move to grammar practice, then use the audio cards for listening, noticing, and speaking.
📐 Grammar — Present Simple for Companies ▼
Subject Positive Negative
The company The company exports software. The company does not export cars.
She / He She manages the sales team. He doesn't work in finance.
We / They We have offices in Asia. They don't sell direct to customers.
Remember: Add -s or -es to the verb with he / she / it. In company descriptions, this usually appears with jobs, departments, and routines.
The company sells medical equipment.
The sales team works from Monday to Friday.
Our head office is in Singapore.

Question forms

Do / Does questions are common in this unit.
Does the company have a finance department?
Do you work in the main office?
Does your company have branches overseas?
💼 Vocabulary — Companies & Departments ▼
company
an organization that sells goods or services
"She works for a small company."
office
a place where people work
"The office is on the third floor."
head office
the main office of a company
"Our head office is in Bangkok."
branch
a local office in another place
"We have a branch in Hanoi."
department
a section of a company
"She works in the HR department."
sales
the team that sells products
"Sales are very strong this month."
finance
money, accounts, and budgets
"Finance checks the numbers."
HR
human resources
"HR recruits new staff."
production
making products
"The production team works fast."
service
the work a company provides
"They provide a delivery service."
factory
a place where products are made
"The factory is outside the city."
subsidiary
a smaller company owned by a larger one
"The subsidiary is in Japan."
Our company is a small software company.
We have a sales team, a finance department, and customer service.
The head office is in Bangkok and the branch office is in Chiang Mai.

💬 Conversation Practice ▼
Prompt 1: Describe your company in 3 sentences.
Prompt 2: Say what departments your company has.
Prompt 3: Ask a partner where their office is located.
Prompt 4: Explain one thing your company does every day.
Prompt 5: Say whether your company has branches, a head office, or a factory.
📝 Quick Review ▼

1. Choose the correct sentence

A) The company export to Japan.
B) The company exports to Japan.
C) The company exporting to Japan.

2. Choose the correct question

A) Does the company have HR?
B) Do the company have HR?
C) Does the company has HR?

3. Fill in the blank

Our sales team ___ new clients.
Answer: meets / supports / calls

4. Review question

What department handles money and budgets?
Answer: finance
