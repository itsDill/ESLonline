🎤 Quick Practice - Conversation at Oxford Phonics 3 Level
1. How are you?
Try answering: "I am fine, thank you. How are you?"
2. How is the weather today?
Practice: "The weather is sunny/rainy/cloudy today."
3. What is your favourite colour?
Answer: "My favourite colour is [blue/red/green/yellow/purple]."
4. How was school today?
Try: "School was good/fun/interesting today."
5. What did you eat for lunch?
Answer: "I ate [rice/chicken/vegetables/noodles] for lunch."
6. Do you like playing sports?
Say: "Yes, I like playing [football/basketball/badminton]."
7. Tell me about your family.
Practice: "I have a mother, father, and [siblings]. My family is [adjective]."
8. What is your hobby?
Answer: "My hobby is [reading/drawing/playing games/sports]."
9. What time do you wake up?
Practice: "I wake up at 7 o'clock in the morning."
10. Can you describe your bedroom?
Try: "My bedroom is [big/small/bright]. It has a bed, desk, and [other items]."
11. What did you do yesterday?
Say: "Yesterday, I [verb]... For example, I played with my friends."
12. What is your dream job?
Answer: "I want to be a [doctor/teacher/engineer] because [reason]."
