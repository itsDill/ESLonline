🏠 Recommended Home Practice
Things to practice between lessons
  • Practice 5 sentences using "He / She + verb + s" (e.g. "My dad drives a car. My mum cooks rice.")
  • Play the Flashcard game on ESL Fun Online — Vocabulary section — for 5 minutes, 3 times a week
  • Watch one short English video about robots or space and tell the teacher one new word next lesson
  • Say 3 sentences each morning in English about what you are doing: "I am eating breakfast. I am wearing…"
🎯 Next Session Goals
  • Introduce Past Simple with irregular verbs (go → went, see → saw) using a space adventure story
  • Conversation drill: "Tell me about your weekend" — full paragraph practice
  • Quick Quiz: Grammar review game (Simple Present vs Present Continuous)
