📝 Report Writing Best Practices

Structure & Format

  • Use clear headings for easy navigation
  • Lead with key points and conclusions
  • Include visual elements (charts, graphs)
  • Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences max)

Writing Style

  • Be concise and eliminate unnecessary words
  • Use active voice ("We completed" vs "Was completed")
  • Include specific data with numbers and percentages
  • Avoid jargon and write for your audience

Professional Language

  • "Analysis indicates..." instead of "We think..."
  • "Sales increased 15%" instead of "Sales were good"
  • "Three critical issues" instead of "Some problems"
  • Use data-driven statements
