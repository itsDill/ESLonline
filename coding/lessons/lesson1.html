What is Programming?

Programming is like giving instructions to a computer. Just like you follow instructions to build LEGO or bake a cake, computers follow instructions written by programmers!

ü§î Why Do We Need Programming?

Computers are very fast but they don't know what to do on their own. They need clear, step-by-step instructions. When you write these instructions, you're programming!

üçï Real Life Example: Making a Sandwich

Imagine telling a robot to make a sandwich. You need to be very specific:

  1. Open the bread bag
  2. Take out two slices of bread
  3. Put peanut butter on one slice
  4. Put jelly on the other slice
  5. Put the slices together

That's exactly how programming works!
