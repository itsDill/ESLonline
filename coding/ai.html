üìÖ The Evolution of AI

Travel through time and see how AI developed from simple ideas to today's amazing technology!

1950s - The Birth of AI

Alan Turing asked "Can machines think?" and created the famous Turing Test. The term "Artificial Intelligence" was coined at a conference at Dartmouth College.

1960s-70s - Early AI Programs

Scientists created programs that could play checkers and chess, solve algebra problems, and even understand simple conversations in English!

1980s-90s - Neural Networks

Researchers developed neural networks inspired by the human brain. Expert systems started being used in businesses and hospitals.

1997 - Deep Blue Beats Chess Champion

IBM's Deep Blue computer defeated world chess champion Garry Kasparov, showing that AI could beat humans at complex strategic games!

2011 - Siri Arrives

Apple introduced Siri, bringing voice-activated AI assistants to millions of people's pockets. Talking to AI became normal!

2010s - Self-Driving Cars

Companies like Tesla and Google started testing cars that can drive themselves using AI and sensors. The future of transportation began!

2020s - ChatGPT & Modern AI

Advanced AI like ChatGPT can write, create art, code programs, and have natural conversations. AI is everywhere and helping everyone!
