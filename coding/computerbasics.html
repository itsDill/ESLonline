A computer is like a super smart helper that can remember things, do math really fast, and help us learn and play!

What Makes a Computer? üñ•Ô∏è

Super Easy!

A computer has different parts that work together, just like how your body has a brain, eyes, and hands that work together to help you!

  • Brain (CPU) - thinks and remembers
  • Eyes (Monitor) - shows you pictures
  • Hands (Keyboard & Mouse) - lets you talk to it
  • Memory (Storage) - keeps your photos and games

  • Desktop - Big computer at home or school

  • Phone - Tiny computer in your pocket!

  • Play fun games and puzzles

  • Talk to family and friends

The Internet - Worldwide Friends! üåê

Everywhere!

The Internet connects all computers around the world! It's like a huge invisible road that lets computers share information and talk to each other.

  • WiFi connects us without wires
  • Search for anything you want to know
  • Send messages instantly
  • Watch videos from anywhere

üîß Computer Parts - Like Body Parts!

Let's learn about computer parts by comparing them to your body parts. It's fun and easy to remember!

CPU - Computer's Brain üß†

Thinking Power!

The CPU is like the computer's brain! Just like your brain helps you think and solve problems, the CPU helps the computer think and do math super fast!

  • Thinks super fast - millions of times per second!
  • Does math problems instantly
  • Solves problems and makes decisions
  • Controls all other computer parts

Monitor - Computer's Eyes üëÄ

See Everything!

The monitor (screen) is like the computer's way of showing you things! Just like your eyes let you see, the monitor lets you see what the computer is thinking!

  • Shows millions of pretty colors
  • Displays pictures and videos
  • Shows text and words you can read
  • Can be big or small screens

Keyboard & Mouse - Computer's Hands ‚úã

Touch & Type!

The keyboard and mouse are like the computer's hands! Just like you use your hands to touch and move things, you use these to "touch" and move things on the computer!

  • Type letters, numbers, and words
  • Point and click on things
  • Move around and select items
  • Make things happen with clicks and taps!

Storage - Computer's Memory üíæ

Never Forget!

Storage is like the computer's memory box! Just like you remember your favorite toy or song, computers use storage to remember your photos, games, and files!

  • Keeps all your photos and videos safe
  • Stores your favorite games
  • Remembers your music and sounds
  • Saves your school work and documents

üõ°Ô∏è Stay Safe Online!

Just like we have rules to stay safe in real life, we have rules to stay safe when using computers and the internet!

Keep Personal Info Private ü§ê

Never share your real name, address, phone number, or school name with strangers online!

Use Strong Passwords üîê

Create passwords with letters, numbers, and symbols. Don't share passwords with anyone except parents!

Ask for Help üôã

If something online makes you feel scared or confused, always ask a trusted adult for help!

Be Kind Online üíù

Treat others nicely on the internet, just like you would in person. No mean words or bullying!
