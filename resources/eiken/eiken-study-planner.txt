=====================================================
STUDY TIPS:
=====================================================

1. CONSISTENCY > INTENSITY
   Study a little every day rather than cramming

2. USE MULTIPLE METHODS
   Reading, writing, listening, speaking

3. REVIEW REGULARLY
   Revisit old material, not just new

4. PRACTICE UNDER TEST CONDITIONS
   Time yourself, no distractions

5. SLEEP WELL
   Your brain consolidates learning during sleep

6. STAY POSITIVE
   Confidence helps performance!
